Intro

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a dedicated Data Analyst with a passion for transforming data into meaningful insights. Here, you'll find a showcase of my work, skills, and the unique approach I bring to the world of data analytics. Here are my awesome projects.

Hospital Operations Analysis

This project focuses on optimizing hospital operations by analyzing patient admissions, resource allocation, and stay durations using Excel Power Pivot and Power BI. Key insights include high trauma cases in gynecology and inefficiencies in ward resource use, leading to actionable recommendations for improving patient care and operational efficiency.

Bank Customer Churn ANalysis

In this project, I used Tableau to find out why customers were leaving a bank. I looked at customer data from different angles, like age and how much they use the bank, and created two easy-to-understand dashboards.

I found that things like a customer's age and how often they use the bank's services are important in understanding why they might leave. This information is really useful for banks to help keep their customers happy and loyal. Click here to view the dashboard. and full report.

Shopping Trend Dashboard

Leveraged Power BI for a comprehensive analysis of consumer shopping data, aiming to uncover key demographic and spending trends.

Identified a preference for clothing and digital payments, with moderate impact from discounts. Recommendations for targeted marketing strategies were provided.

Customer Behavior Dashboard

In this project, I developed an interactive dashboard using Tableau to visualize and analyze customer demographic and purchase data. The aim was to uncover the factors that most significantly affect purchasing decisions.

Revealed key purchase trends among younger customers and higher earners, guiding targeted marketing initiatives.

About

Hi, I’m a Data Analyst with a year of experience turning data into insights that help businesses make better decisions. I work with tools like Power BI, Excel, and SQL to analyze and visualize data, making it easier for people to understand and act on. I enjoy creating dashboards, automating reports, and solving data-related problems to improve efficiency.

I’ve had the chance to work on different projects, from analyzing customer behavior to improving operations in hospitals. My goal is always to find meaningful patterns in the data and turn them into actions that drive results.

I have a Graduate Certificate in Big Data Analytics from Georgian College and a Bachelor’s degree in Philosophy from Pontifical Urbaniana University. This combination has taught me how to think critically and approach challenges from different angles.

I’m also really interested in machine learning and AI and how they can be used to make even more sense of the data we collect.

Outside of work, I love playing the guitar, listening to music, going for walks, and engaing in creative writing.
